DATE: 2008 // LOCATION: Havenwood, CO (Pop. 8,500), adjacent to the Rocky Mountain National Park. This region is designated a high-priority "Veil" zone due to its unique geophysical properties and high incidence of anomalous biological entities (ABEs). ENTITY ID: L-02A (WEREWOLF)

CLASSIFICATION: LYCANTHROPIC AGENT (WEREWOLF)
Description / Profile

Subject is a human infected with a heavily mutated strain of canine pathogen, hypothesized to be an ancient, stable variant of the Rabies virus. The infection causes irreversible cellular restructuring during cyclical transformation, which occurs every four (4) days. Contrary to folklore, transformation is NOT moon-dependent.

Threat Assessment
Designation: LUPINE HOST Harm Rating: 8 (Physical - Severe) Vulnerability: Specialized Pathogen Dispersal, Physical Trauma (Decapitation, Massive Organ Failure) Reported Sector: Wilderness Borders / Isolated Properties
Recorded Movements (Abilities)
  • PATHOGENESIS: Infection is transmitted via direct fluid exchange (bites/scratches). Highly aggressive, but aggression levels vary post-transformation.
  • TRANSFORMATION (CYCLICAL): Occurs every 96 hours post-infection. Form varies from large humanoids to full-wolf forms. Subject retains little to no memory of the shift.
  • RESILIENCE: Standard firearms are largely ineffective. Silver-based weaponry is a traditional countermeasure but is currently unverified by R&D.
Protocol & Countermeasures
  • PROTOCOL 7-B: DO NOT attempt verbal communication or emotional de-escalation; the subject is neurologically compromised.
  • COUNTERMEASURE: Lethal force is authorized. Prioritize non-contact neutralization via high-velocity rounds or explosive trauma.
  • TREATMENT: No known cure for the irreversible cellular changes. Containment is the only option.
Plot Hooks & Mission Briefs
  • A local hiker went missing near an old, abandoned mill. Their body was found with signs of a severe animal attack, but forensics are inconclusive.
  • Reports of a large, unidentified animal are coming from a remote farmstead. The farmer claims it's been stealing livestock every few nights, like clockwork.

ENTITY ID: S-11B (VAMPIRE)

CLASSIFICATION: SANGUINE INFILTRATOR (VAMPIRE)
Description / Profile

Humanoid entity that is biologically alive but requires consistent ingestion of human blood for metabolic function. Cannot consume solid foods. Displays no aging or signs of common disease. Often mistaken for other non-pathological criminals, allowing for widespread population dispersal.

  • MENTAL DOMINION: Subject can assert subtle forms of mental influence on humans (hypnotic suggestion).
  • ENHANCED SENSES: Exceptional night vision (eyes glow in low light) and superior olfactory detection (can track blood scent over long distances).
  • INOCULATION: Can infect others via fluid exchange. A 'soft-cure' exists to prevent further infection, but the host changes are irreversible.
Protocol & Countermeasures
  • PROTOCOL 1-A: Lethal force is authorized, but the subject can be killed by any means that would kill a normal human. Target central nervous system.
  • COUNTERMEASURE: Garlic extract dispersed as a fine mist causes uncontrollable spasms and aggression, disrupting Mental Dominion.
  • R&D NOTE: Fangs are highly durable and continuously grow if not naturally worn down by host activities.
Plot Hooks & Mission Briefs

ENTITY ID: CH-41E (GORGON)

ENTITY ID: ABC-01 (DRAGON)

Description / Profile

It possesses specialized, paired pyrophoric glands that synthesize and store highly unstable, auto-igniting chemicals (similar to phosphine). The 'fire' is the result of forcefully ejecting and mixing these compounds upon contact with air.

Threat Assessment
Designation: FIRE-BREATHER Harm Rating: 10 (Environmental/Mass Destruction) Vulnerability: Cryogenic Agents, Internal Pressure Disruption, Water Immersion (Extremely High Volume) Reported Sector: Remote Caverns / Active Geothermal Zones
Recorded Movements (Abilities)
  • THERMAL INJECTION: Capable of incinerating targets from 50m. Thermal plume reaches 1,500°C. Requires time to replenish chemical stores after a prolonged attack.
  • ARMORED HIDE: Epidermal plates are reinforced with high-density keratin, making them highly resistant to standard high-explosive rounds.
  • FLIGHT: Possesses large, complex musculature for atmospheric lift. Observed to be agile despite size.
Protocol & Countermeasures
  • PROTOCOL 10-D: Only engage with anti-tank or specialized cryogenic weapons systems. Standard neutralization is not possible.
  • COUNTERMEASURE: Target the vent-like structures behind the mandibles; these are critical for chemical pressure regulation.
  • R&D NOTE: The ABC's core weakness is its massive caloric and hydration needs; prolonged engagement should aim to exhaust its internal reserves.
Plot Hooks & Mission Briefs
