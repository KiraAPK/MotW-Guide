ENTITY ID: L-02A (WEREWOLF)

CLASSIFICATION: LYCANTHROPIC AGENT (WEREWOLF)
Description / Profile

Subject is a human infected with a heavily mutated strain of canine pathogen, hypothesized to be an ancient, stable variant of the Rabies virus. The infection causes irreversible cellular restructuring during cyclical transformation, which occurs every four (4) days. Contrary to folklore, transformation is NOT moon-dependent.

Threat Assessment
Designation: LUPINE HOST Harm Rating: 8 (Physical - Severe) Vulnerability: Specialized Pathogen Dispersal, Physical Trauma (Decapitation, Massive Organ Failure) Reported Sector: Wilderness Borders / Isolated Properties
Recorded Movements (Abilities)
  • PATHOGENESIS: Infection is transmitted via direct fluid exchange (bites/scratches). Highly aggressive, but aggression levels vary post-transformation.
  • TRANSFORMATION (CYCLICAL): Occurs every 96 hours post-infection. Form varies from large humanoids to full-wolf forms. Subject retains little to no memory of the shift.
  • RESILIENCE: Standard firearms are largely ineffective. Silver-based weaponry is a traditional countermeasure but is currently unverified by R&D.
Protocol & Countermeasures
  • PROTOCOL 7-B: DO NOT attempt verbal communication or emotional de-escalation; the subject is neurologically compromised.
  • COUNTERMEASURE: Lethal force is authorized. Prioritize non-contact neutralization via high-velocity rounds or explosive trauma.
  • TREATMENT: No known cure for the irreversible cellular changes. Containment is the only option.
Plot Hooks & Mission Briefs
