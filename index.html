ENTITY ID: V-98C (VAMPIRE)

CLASSIFICATION: HIGH-RISK INFECTIOUS VECTOR - DISPERSAL THREAT
Description / Profile (SANGUINE INFILTRATOR)

Subject is a living humanoid entity that sustains itself exclusively on whole blood, possessing superior physical durability against common diseases and displaying zero biological aging. Mythological accounts are highly inaccurate; subject possesses no shapeshifting or levitation capabilities and is NOT biologically 'undead.' The primary threat is the subject's ability to easily blend into the population and rapidly create new vectors through fluid exchange (infection).

Threat Assessment
Designation: SANGUINE INFILTRATOR Harm Rating: 8 (Exsanguination/Psychological) Vulnerability: Aconite Fumes (Garlic), Blood Deprivation (14 days) Vector: Saliva/Blood exchange (Infection Rate: HIGH) Cure Status: NO FULL CURE. Soft-cure available to prevent further transmission.
Recorded Movements (Abilities)
  • MENTAL DOMINION: Subject can assert subtle mental influence over low-willpower humans, used for social manipulation or momentary distraction.
  • NOCTURNAL ACUITY: Excellent low-light vision. Subject's retinas reflect light (cat-like glowing eyes) when exposed to dim illumination.
  • OLFACTORY TRIGGER: The presence of open blood causes an immediate, aggressive feeding response, bypassing human rationale.
  • BIOLOGICAL IMPULSE: Subject experiences extreme mental instability and aggression after 7 days without consuming blood.
Protocol & Countermeasures
  • PROTOCOL 4-D: Standard lethal force is authorized. The subject can be neutralized by any means capable of killing a human being.
  • COUNTERMEASURE: Exposure to aerosolized Aconite (Garlic) fumes causes intense spasms and uncontrollable aggression, potentially incapacitating the subject briefly.
  • HANDLING WARNING: Fangs are continuously growing and highly durable. DO NOT attempt close-quarters capture without proper neck/limb protection. Reasoning is not recommended; the subject is highly opportunistic.

ENTITY ID: L-02A (WEREWOLF)

CLASSIFICATION: BIOLOGICAL HAZARD - TERMINATE ON SIGHT
Description / Profile (LYCANTHROPIC AGENT)

Subject is an individual infected with a highly mutated, Rabies-like pathogen believed to originate from a non-native wolf strain. This infection causes irreversible physical and neurological changes, resulting in cyclical, aggressive transformations. The phase of the moon has no correlation with the transformation cycle. Subjects generally operate independently and avoid congregation. NOTE: Transformation forms are highly variable, ranging from hyper-muscled humanoids to large, almost pure-canine predators. Subjects exhibit profound retrograde amnesia post-shift.

Threat Assessment
Designation: LYCANTHROPIC AGENT Harm Rating: 10+ (Unstable/Rending) Transformation Cycle: Predictable Shift every 96 hours (4 Days) Reported Vector: Infection through deep bite or fluid exchange. Status: NO KNOWN CURE. Changes are irreversible.
Transformation Triggers & Abilities
  • BIOLOGICAL IMPULSE: Transformation is automatically triggered 96 hours post-last shift.
  • ACCELERATED SHIFT: Transformation can be triggered early by extreme physiological stress (e.g., severe injury, adrenaline spike, or intense fear).
  • SUPPRESSED MEMORY: Subject has no conscious memory of activities during the transformed state.
  • PATHOGENIC RESISTANCE: Subject is highly resistant to non-silver, non-magical blunt force trauma.
Protocol & Countermeasures
  • PROTOCOL 3-C (SEEK & DESTROY): Containment is not recommended. Engagement must prioritize rapid neutralization.
  • COUNTERMEASURE: Weaponry must utilize pure silver components for maximum biological trauma. Standard ammunition is ineffective for immediate cessation.
  • HANDLING WARNING: DO NOT attempt verbal de-escalation or appeal to the human personality during a transformation or shift cycle. Aggression is mandatory and non-negotiable.

ENTITY ID: G-33P (GORGON)

CLASSIFICATION: IRREVERSIBLE BIO-HAZARD - CONTAINMENT PRIORITY
Description / Profile (CHIMERIC OBSERVER)

The Gorgon is an entity characterized by a highly rare physiological trait: the ability to induce immediate, irreversible petrification in living tissue via direct eye contact. Pureblood specimens are extremely rare and poorly documented. Most encounters involve hybrids, who are visually indistinguishable from humans unless displaying secondary traits (e.g., scale remnants). They do not possess shapeshifting or other metamorphic abilities. If trained from a young age, the subject can learn to suppress the petrification effect.

Threat Assessment
Designation: CHIMERIC OBSERVER Harm Rating: 10 (Irreversible Systemic Trauma) Vulnerability: Sight Occlusion (Absolute), Ocular Trauma Population Density: Extremely Low (Rare Pureblood, Uncommon Hybrid) Engagement Risk: HIGH (Due to one-hit incapacitation ability)
Recorded Movements (Abilities)
  • PETRIFYING GAZE: Primary function. Upon sustained, direct, line-of-sight eye contact, the subject can biochemically transform the target's tissue into a silicate mass.
  • CONTROLLED STASIS: Trained Gorgons can consciously turn the gaze off or on, often through intense focus or learned visual cues (Requires years of training).
  • UNTRAINED SHIFT: Untrained or highly stressed hybrids may unconsciously trigger the gaze when experiencing extreme fear or panic, making them unpredictable.
Protocol & Countermeasures
  • PROTOCOL 5-A (SIGHT MANAGEMENT): Immediate, mandatory application of reinforced, non-reflective ocular restraints (BLINDFOLD) upon capture or potential containment.
  • COUNTERMEASURE: Engage only using reflective surfaces (mirrors) to monitor location, or use indirect fire / high-level flashbangs to temporarily impair visual focus before neutralization.
  • HANDLING WARNING: The effect is purely visual. Subjects can be moved and handled safely provided their eyes are fully covered and the restraint cannot be compromised. Target the optic nerves if lethal force is required.
